Class Player
Collaborators: Account, QRCode, Profile
Responsibilities: 
	- Adds QR Codes
	- Removes QR codes
	- Comments on QR Codes
	- Browses own account
	- Browses other player accounts
	- Generates game status QR codes
	- Generates QR code to log in from another device 


Class Account
Collaborators: QRCode, Player
Responsibilities:
	- Store QR codes
	- Display QR codes

Class Stats
Collaborators: Account, QRCode
Responsibilities: 
	- Find highest and lowest QR code
	- Find sum of QR code
	- Find total number of QR codes

Class QRCode

	- Has score
	- Has geolocation
	- Has photo of object/location
